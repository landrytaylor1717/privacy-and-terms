1Introduction

RuleBase ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to safeguarding your personal information while providing an engaging trivia and rulebook experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Disclaimer: RuleBase is an independent platform not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to any professional sports leagues, teams, associations, or governing bodies. All rulebook content is derived from publicly available information and may represent summarized or paraphrased interpretations of official rules for educational and entertainment purposes. For official or authoritative rule interpretations, users should consult the governing body of the respective sport.

2Information We Collect

2.1 Account & Profile Information

  • Name (required for public leaderboard or profile)
  • Email address (for login and support)
  • Authentication method (Google, Apple, Email)
  • Biography, favorite sport/team, and trivia level
  • IP address, session identifiers, device-specific IDs

2.2 Gameplay & App Activity

  • Trivia performance (correct answers, stats, progress)
  • Leaderboard rank, history of games played
  • Friends list, matchmaking interactions
  • In-app ratings and feedback

2.3 Device & Technical Data

  • Device model, OS version, app version
  • App usage logs, crashes, performance metrics
  • Notification tokens (if enabled)

2.4 Speech Input (Optional)

  • Transcripts for voice-based answers using Google Cloud Speech API (not stored beyond session)

2.5 Support & Communication

  • Name and contact when submitting support requests
  • Message content, related metadata (device, timestamp)

3Third-Party Services

We partner with trusted platforms to power our features. These may access or temporarily store your data:

  • Firebase (Google LLC): Auth, Firestore DB, Storage, Analytics
  • Google Cloud: Speech recognition, hosting
  • Typesense: Rule and user profile search
  • OpenAI: Trivia question generation, content moderation
  • Pinecone: Personalized AI-driven trivia recommendations
  • Render & Railway: App and server hosting
  • Expo/AsyncStorage: Push notifications, local storage
  • GitHub: Internal code hosting (no user-facing access)

4How We Use Your Data

  • Deliver core features (trivia, matchmaking, rule search)
  • Personalize game difficulty and recommendations
  • Maintain public rankings and user content
  • Moderate reported content and user behavior
  • Analyze performance and app improvements
  • Communicate with you (opt-in communications only)
  • Comply with legal requirements

5Public Information Visibility

Your name, bio, favorite team/sport, and stats may be visible to other users. You can update or remove these anytime.

6Information Sharing

We do not sell your data. Sharing is limited to:

  • Service providers listed in Section 3
  • Legal or regulatory bodies upon valid request
  • Internal moderation teams (to investigate reports)
  • Other users (e.g., usernames in leaderboards)

7Content Moderation

To protect our community, we use a combination of:

  • AI moderation tools (e.g., OpenAI, Google)
  • Human review for reported content
  • Automated filters for usernames, bios, and chats

Offensive content may result in suspension or permanent ban.

8Data Storage & Retention

  • Data is stored securely in Firebase, Typesense, and Pinecone
  • Voice transcripts are transient and not retained
  • Your data remains until you request deletion or close your account
  • We retain necessary information to comply with legal obligations

9Security Measures

  • Encrypted connections (SSL/TLS)
  • Tokenized access and Firebase security rules
  • Limited and audited access by staff and moderators

No system is 100% immune to breaches. Protect your account with a secure password.

10Children's Privacy

RuleBase is not intended for users under 13 (or under 16 depending on jurisdiction). If we discover we've collected data from a minor without appropriate consent, we'll promptly delete it.

11Your Rights

You may request to:

  • View, update, or delete your personal data
  • Export your user data
  • Opt out of analytics or notifications
  • Report inappropriate content or appeal moderation actions

12International Users

Your data may be processed in the U.S. or other countries with different data laws. By using RuleBase, you consent to such transfers.
